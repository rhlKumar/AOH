1. About our services

Online Academic Help is an academic consultancy based in India. We provide guidance, mentoring, research support, and editorial services to doctoral and research scholars. Our services are advisory and supportive in nature and are intended to help you develop and complete your own original work.

2. Academic integrity

We provide mentoring and editorial assistance, not ready-made academic submissions. We do not write theses to be submitted as your own, sit examinations, fabricate data, or guarantee any grade, degree, publication, or examination outcome. You are responsible for ensuring that your use of our services complies with your institution's academic integrity and ethics policies. You agree to use our guidance as a learning aid in the development of your own work.

3. Your responsibilities

  • Ensure that the work and data you provide do not infringe anyone else's rights;

The initial consultation is free and carries no charge or refund.
